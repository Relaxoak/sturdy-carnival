The Little Mermaid is credited with starting the Disney Renaissance and breathing new life into animated feature films.

The film was later adapted into a stage production with new songs and a book, which opened in Denver in 2007 and later on Broadway in 2008.

At the time of writing (2018) Disney are also developing a life action version of the movie.
